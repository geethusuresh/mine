About Oneconsole

OneConsole™ is a unified service and operations management Platform providing single pane of glass for Enterprises to provision, monitor, manage and govern IT services on-premise or in cloud. Its provisioning module is Hybrid Cloud enabled which can deploy applications to on-premise or public cloud. Its helpdesk system is highly collaborative and provides better user experience of interacting with IT. Its cognitive knowledge engine helps both users and IT support team to find solutions faster.

OneConsole™ has the ability to learn IT operations and business service performance patterns, providing better analytics and anomaly predictions. Embedded with security and governance, you can be best assured that the IT systems are compliant and fortified wherever it is deployed.
