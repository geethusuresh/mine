 Incidents

Case Type Priority Description Response SLA or Resolution SLA
Incident Tickets (Critical) Incidents having major impact on the production environment and/or impacting mission critical business functions.Complete interruption of service, system, network, application or configuration item leading complete business loss. There is no possible alternative Response SLA <= 15 Min
  • Updates - Flash Report Frequency : Every 1 Hours until closure
  • Internal Escalation - RIMS Leadership Team
  • External Escalation - Customer Authorized contact, Customer Team, Customer Service Account Manager
Resolution SLA <= 4 Hours
Incident Tickets (High) Incidents having severe impact on business and /or production environment. A Large Group of end-users are being impacted and/or business functions with limited functionality. No acceptable alternative is possible. Response SLA <= 30 Min
Resolution SLA <= 6 Hours
Incident Tickets (Medium) Incidents having minor impact on production environment and/or business. Allowing standard day to day business operations. A workaround is available. Response SLA <= 60 Min
Resolution SLA <= 24 Hours
Incident Ticket (Low) Incidents having no impact on production and/or business. An event which is an inconvenience for the user, for which there is an alternative or a possible remedy but does not prevent the user from working. Response SLA <= 120 Min
Resolution SLA <= 48 Hours
