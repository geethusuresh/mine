What is OneConsole ?

One Console portal enables the Marlabs internal and Managed Services customers to login and avail the services offered by Marlabs Infrastructure service team. The portal is offering various services like helpdesk, Asset Management, Security and Infrastructure monitoring, analytics dashboards and cloud and platform services to Marlabs customers.

All Marlabs Employees will have access to Helpdesk module of the oneconsole tool. The helpdesk module of oneconsole enables the users to contact the helpdesk and avail services Infrastructure support team.
